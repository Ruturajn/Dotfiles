# This File contains the keybindings for various tasks (For Qtile WM)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| S.No. | Task                                          | Keybinding                  |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 1.    | Launch Rofi                                   | Control + Shift + L         |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 2.    | Launch Terminal                               | Super + Return              |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 3.    | Reload Qtile config                           | Super + Control + R         |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 4.    | Toggle Focus between Open Windows             | Super + Space               |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 5.    | Move Focus between Windows                    | Super + h/j/k/l (Vim based) |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 6.    | Toggle Tiling Layout                          | Super + Tab                 |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 7.    | Close a Window                                | Super + W                   |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 8.    | Resize the Tiled windows in 'columns' layout  | Super + Control + h/j/k/l   |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 9.    | Launch Flameshot                              | Super + Control + S         |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 10.   | Toggle Mute Speaker                           | XF86AudioMute               |                 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 11.   | Decrease Speaker volume                       | XF86AudioLowerVolume        |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 12.   | Increase Speaker volume                       | XF86AudioRaiseVolume        |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 13.   | Launch Browser (Firefox)                      | Super + B                   |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 14.   | Launch File Manager (nemo)                    | Super + F                   |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 15.   | See CPU Fan Speed as a dunst Notification     | Alt + F                     |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 16.   | See Tempeartures for CPU cores (dunst)        | Alt + C                     |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 17.   | Increase Screen Brightness                    | XF86MonBrightnessUp         |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 18.   | Decrease Screen Brightness                    | XF86MonBrightnessDown       |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 19.   | Toggle Music Play-Pause                       | XF86AudioPlay               |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 20.   | Play the previous Music Track                 | XF86AudioPrev               |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 21.   | Play the next Music Track                     | XF86AudioNext               |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 22.   | Stop Playing the Music Track                  | XF86AudioStop               |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 23.   | Show the Power Menu                           | Super + Shift + Q           |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 24.   | Show the Wi-Fi Menu                           | Alt + W                     |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Note: The XF86* Keys are generally fn+<some_F<number>_Key>. They will usually have
some sort of icons on it depicting it's function.
